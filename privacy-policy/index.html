This policy describes how BlueHarvest Private Limited ("BlueHarvest Exchange," "we," "us," or "our") handles personal information when you visit our website, use our offline marketplace services, or interact with us for business purposes. This document is provided for transparency and does not constitute legal advice.

1. Introduction

BlueHarvest Exchange operates a government-licensed seafood trading platform connecting verified suppliers with institutional and commercial buyers in India. Protecting the confidentiality and integrity of personal and business information is essential to maintaining trust in our marketplace.

By accessing our website or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue use of our website and services and contact us with any concerns.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate our marketplace, verify participants, process transactions, and communicate with users. The categories of information may include:

2.1 Personal and business information

  • Full name, designation, and contact details (email address, telephone number, business address)
  • Company or trading entity name, registration details, and tax identification numbers
  • Account credentials such as username and authentication data
  • Banking and payment details required to settle marketplace transactions
  • Licences, certifications, and compliance documents submitted by suppliers or buyers

2.2 Usage and technical information

  • Pages visited, features used, and interaction with our website
  • Device type, browser, operating system, and IP address
  • Log files, session data, and diagnostic information
  • Transaction history and marketplace activity records

2.3 Communications

  • Correspondence with our support, sales, or compliance teams
  • Records of enquiries submitted through contact forms, email, or telephone

3. How We Collect Information

We obtain information through the following methods:

  • Directly from you when you register an account, submit forms, enter into agreements, or communicate with us
  • Automatically when you browse our website, including through cookies and analytics tools
  • From third parties such as payment processors, verification partners, regulatory authorities, or business references where permitted by law

4. How We Use Information

We use personal information for specific, legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Providing, operating, and improving our offline and online trading services
  • Verifying the identity and eligibility of marketplace participants
  • Processing transactions, settlements, and related financial operations
  • Communicating service updates, account notices, and operational messages
  • Responding to support requests and resolving disputes
  • Monitoring platform integrity, preventing fraud, and ensuring regulatory compliance
  • Analysing usage trends to improve website performance and user experience
  • Sending marketing or promotional communications where you have provided consent

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Our processing of personal data is carried out in accordance with applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), and other relevant regulations. Depending on the context, we rely on one or more of the following bases:

  • Consent — where you have given clear permission, such as for optional marketing communications
  • Contractual necessity — where processing is required to perform our agreement with you or take pre-contractual steps at your request
  • Legal obligation — where we must comply with applicable laws, licensing requirements, or lawful requests from authorities
  • Legitimate interests — where processing is necessary for secure platform operations, fraud prevention, or service improvement, balanced against your rights

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information only where necessary and subject to appropriate safeguards:

  • With counterparties in a transaction, limited to information required to complete the trade
  • With service providers who assist with hosting, analytics, email delivery, payment processing, or compliance verification
  • With financial institutions involved in payment settlement
  • With professional advisers bound by confidentiality obligations
  • With regulators, courts, or law enforcement when required by applicable law

All third-party processors are expected to handle data securely and only for authorised purposes. Cross-border transfers, if any, will be conducted in compliance with applicable data protection requirements.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Retention periods may vary based on:

  • The duration of your business relationship with BlueHarvest Exchange
  • Statutory record-keeping obligations for financial and regulatory compliance
  • The need to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

When data is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it securely.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request erasure of personal information where legally permitted
  • Withdraw consent for processing that relies on consent
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in India

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

9. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain basic functionality and understand how visitors use our site. We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated usage statistics. This may include page views, session duration, and general device or browser information.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Where required by law, we will obtain consent before placing non-essential cookies.

10. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include access controls, encryption where appropriate, staff training, and periodic review of our security practices.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children's Privacy

BlueHarvest Exchange provides business-to-business marketplace services and does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.
