Our Story

Learn about our journey to transform India's fish trading landscape

BlueHarvestExchange was established in 2024 with a vision to transform India's fragmented fish trading landscape. Founded by a team of seafood industry experts, supply chain specialists, and technology innovators, our platform addresses the critical challenges facing the sector.

BlueHarvestExchange is India's new standard in fish trading — a government-licensed seafood marketplace that connects fishermen, suppliers, and buyers through efficient, transparent, and scalable systems.

We currently operate as an offline trading hub in key coastal regions and are preparing to launch our full-featured online marketplace and smart trawler support systems in 2025.

Our Mission

To create a transparent, efficient, and sustainable fish trading ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders—from fisherfolk to end consumers—while promoting responsible fishing practices and optimizing the supply chain.

Our Vision

To become India's premier integrated fish trading platform, setting new standards for quality, transparency, and efficiency in the seafood industry.
