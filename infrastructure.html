Cold Chain Excellence

Maintaining the perfect temperature from catch to consumer

Cold Chain

End-to-End Temperature Control

Our comprehensive cold chain infrastructure ensures that seafood remains fresh from the moment it's caught until it reaches the end buyer:

  • Dockside Ice Facilities: Immediate cooling of fresh catch
  • Refrigerated Receiving Areas: Temperature-controlled intake zones
  • Blast Freezers: Rapid freezing technology to lock in freshness
  • Cold Storage Warehouses: Multiple temperature zones for different seafood types
  • Refrigerated Transport: GPS-tracked climate-controlled delivery vehicles
  • Temperature Monitoring: IoT sensors throughout the supply chain

Our facilities maintain temperatures between -22°C and 2°C depending on the product requirements and storage duration needs.

Quality Control Laboratories

Scientific testing to ensure only the best seafood reaches your table

Scientific Quality Assurance

Each BlueHarvestExchange facility features an advanced quality testing laboratory that ensures all products meet the highest standards:

  • Microbiological Testing: Ensuring products are free from harmful bacteria
  • Chemical Analysis: Testing for contaminants and preservatives
  • Sensory Evaluation: Expert assessment of appearance, texture, and freshness
  • Species Verification: DNA testing to confirm fish species authenticity
  • Shelf-life Studies: Determining optimal storage conditions and duration

Our quality control protocols comply with industry standards requirements for seafood safety.

Trading Hub Facilities

Modern marketplaces where buyers and sellers connect directly

Trading Hub

Comprehensive Trading Centers

Our trading hubs are designed to facilitate efficient buying and selling with facilities including:

  • Quality Grading Stations: Visual inspection and grading areas
  • Digital Auction Spaces: Technology-enabled transparent pricing
  • Processing Areas: Basic cleaning and preparation facilities
  • Packaging Zones: Custom packaging solutions for different buyer needs
  • Logistics Office: Coordination of transport and delivery
  • Buyer-Seller Meeting Rooms: Spaces for direct negotiations

Each hub is strategically located near major fishing ports to minimize time between catch and processing.

Processing Capabilities

Value-added processing that meets international standards

Value-Added Processing

For buyers who require processed seafood, we offer various preparation services:

  • Cleaning and Gutting: Basic preparation of whole fish
  • Filleting: Skilled cutting to produce ready-to-cook fillets
  • Portioning: Exact weight cutting for institutional buyers
  • IQF Freezing: Individual quick freezing for premium quality
  • Vacuum Packaging: Extended shelf-life packaging options
  • Modified Atmosphere Packaging: Advanced freshness preservation

All processing is conducted in certified facilities with strict hygiene protocols.
