Core AI Capabilities

Advanced technologies powering our platform's intelligent features

Computer Vision

Computer Vision System

Our advanced computer vision technology revolutionizes seafood quality assessment:

  • Species Identification: Accurately identifies over 200 fish species common in Indian waters
  • Quality Grading: Assesses freshness based on visual characteristics like eye clarity, gill color, and skin condition
  • Size Measurement: Precise dimensional analysis for standardized pricing
  • Defect Detection: Identifies quality issues invisible to the human eye
  • Batch Processing: Analyzes large volumes of seafood simultaneously

The system uses a combination of RGB, infrared, and hyperspectral imaging to deliver comprehensive quality assessments.

Predictive Analytics

Our predictive models provide invaluable market insights to all stakeholders:

  • Price Forecasting: Predicts market prices up to 2 weeks in advance with 85% accuracy
  • Demand Prediction: Forecasts regional demand patterns for different species
  • Seasonal Trends: Identifies upcoming seasonal variations in supply and demand
  • Quality-Price Correlation: Models how quality metrics influence market prices
  • Market Alerts: Generates early warnings about market shifts

These predictive capabilities help fishermen time their harvests, buyers plan their purchases, and logistics providers optimize their operations.

Supply Chain

Supply Chain Optimization

Our AI dynamically optimizes the entire seafood supply chain:

  • Route Optimization: Calculates the most efficient delivery routes based on real-time conditions
  • Inventory Management: Predicts optimal inventory levels to minimize waste
  • Cold Chain Monitoring: Ensures temperature integrity throughout transport
  • Shelf-life Prediction: Estimates remaining shelf-life based on multiple factors
  • Load Balancing: Distributes products optimally across storage facilities

These optimizations reduce costs, minimize waste, and ensure product quality throughout the supply chain.

Smart Trawler System

Our AI revolutionizes operations aboard fishing vessels:

  • On-board Classification: Real-time sorting and grading of catch
  • Catch Documentation: Automated recording of species, volumes, and locations
  • Optimal Fishing Zones: Recommendations based on historical and real-time data
  • Sustainable Fishing: Guidance to avoid overfishing and bycatch
  • Pre-arrival Notifications: Automated alerts to marketplace about incoming catch

This technology transforms traditional fishing operations into data-driven, efficient, and sustainable ventures.
