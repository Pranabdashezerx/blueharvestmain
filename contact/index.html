Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I register as a buyer on BlueHarvest Exchange?

Contact our business development team at +91 7008169612, WhatsApp, or info@blueharvestexchange.com. We will share registration details and guide you through verification, usually within 24 hours.

What documentation is required for sellers?

Sellers need to provide business registration documents, fishing licenses (if applicable), tax identification numbers, and bank account details. Our team will assist you with the verification process when you contact us via phone or email.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, BlueHarvest Exchange offers cold chain logistics to keep seafood fresh in transit. Delivery options and fees depend on location and volume — contact our logistics team for a quote.

Can I visit your facilities before becoming a member?

Absolutely! We encourage potential partners to visit our facilities. You can schedule a tour by contacting our business development team at +91 7008169612. We're proud to showcase our state-of-the-art infrastructure.
