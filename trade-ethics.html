Our Ethical Standards

Principles that guide our trading practices and relationships

At BlueHarvestExchange, we believe that responsible business practices are essential for the long-term sustainability of the seafood industry. Our Trade Ethics Code establishes the principles and standards that guide our operations and relationships with all stakeholders in the seafood supply chain.

This code reflects our commitment to fair trade, environmental sustainability, and social responsibility. All participants in the BlueHarvestExchange marketplace are expected to adhere to these principles.

Fair Business Practices

Our commitment to transparency and fairness

Fair Business

Core Business Principles

We are committed to fair and transparent business practices:

  • Transparent pricing and fee structures
  • Fair compensation for suppliers and fishermen
  • Equal access to marketplace opportunities
  • Prohibition of price manipulation and anti-competitive behavior
  • Timely payments to suppliers

We believe that fair business practices build trust with our partners and create a more sustainable marketplace for everyone involved.

Environmental Sustainability

Our dedication to protecting marine ecosystems

Environmental Commitment

We are dedicated to promoting sustainable fishing practices:

  • Promotion of sustainable fishing practices
  • Compliance with fishing quotas and regulations
  • Reduction of bycatch and marine ecosystem impact
  • Minimization of carbon footprint in logistics operations
  • Commitment to reducing plastic and packaging waste

Social Responsibility

Our pledge to support communities and workers

Social Impact

Social Responsibility Commitment

We are committed to the well-being of people in our supply chain:

  • Zero tolerance for forced labor and human trafficking
  • Fair working conditions throughout the supply chain
  • Support for fishing communities and local economies
  • Respect for workers' rights and safety
  • Commitment to diversity and inclusion

We recognize that the strength of our business depends on the well-being of the communities and individuals who contribute to it.

Product Integrity

Our standards for quality and authenticity

Quality and Integrity Standards

We maintain high standards for product quality and authenticity:

  • Accurate species identification and labeling
  • Truthful representation of product origin and quality
  • Rigorous quality control and food safety standards
  • Complete traceability from catch to consumer
  • Prohibition of seafood fraud and mislabeling

Product integrity is essential for building trust with our customers and ensuring the reputation of our marketplace.

Supplier Code of Conduct

Standards for our supplier partners

Supplier Standards

Supplier Requirements

All suppliers participating in the BlueHarvestExchange marketplace must commit to:

  • Complying with all applicable fishing regulations and licensing requirements
  • Providing accurate documentation of catch methods, locations, and volumes
  • Maintaining proper cold chain management for product quality
  • Ensuring fair labor practices and safe working conditions
  • Implementing measures to reduce environmental impact
  • Participating in sustainability improvement initiatives
  • Allowing verification and audits when requested

Buyer Code of Conduct

Standards for our buyer partners

Buyer Requirements

All buyers participating in the BlueHarvestExchange marketplace must commit to:

  • Honoring agreed-upon contracts and payment terms
  • Providing accurate information about purchase requirements
  • Supporting fair pricing that reflects the true value of sustainable products
  • Respecting supplier relationships and confidentiality
  • Prioritizing suppliers who demonstrate ethical practices
  • Participating in traceability initiatives
  • Reporting any suspected unethical behavior

This Trade Ethics Code was last updated on June 9, 2025, and is reviewed annually by our Ethics Committee.
